About

v1.0.0

A small private web app for a regular check-in with yourself. Runs entirely in your browser — no server, no account, no tracking.

Quick guide

💾
Save

Updates the most recent check-in for today. Use this to refine your entry during the day.

➕
Extra

Adds a new check-in alongside the existing one for today. Useful if your mood or energy shifts significantly.

⚙️
Settings

Choose your language, theme, and which check-in parts are visible. Hidden sections are not deleted — just not shown.

📋
Overview

Browse, search, sort and filter all your saved check-ins. Click a row to open it in the Check-in tab. Export or import entries as JSON.

What you can track

🌡️Weather at check-in time
💭Free-text thoughts
🎡Core feeling via emotion wheel
🧍Body signals on a body figure
⚡Physical, mental & emotional energy levels
🎨Mood via a 10×10 valence/arousal matrix
✅Actions and intentions
📝An optional free-text note

Privacy

✓No server. Everything runs locally in your browser.
✓No account or login required.
✓No analytics, no cookies, no tracking.
✓No personal data is collected or stored.
ℹThe optional weather widget contacts Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com). No personal data is sent — only coordinates or city name.
ℹData is not encrypted by this app.

Storage

✓Your data is stored in your browser localStorage on this device.
✓The app saves entries and settings as plain JSON text in localStorage.
⚠️Opening the app in a different browser or on a different device shows no data — localStorage is not shared.
⚠️Clearing your browser data or cache can permanently delete all your check-ins.
⚠️In private/incognito mode, data is deleted as soon as you close the window.
💾 Back up your data

Use Export in the Overview tab to save a JSON backup. Use Import to restore it or move entries to another browser.

How are the heatmap colours calculated?

The 28-day history and the 7-day summary both use three colours: green (good), orange (mixed) and red (low). The calculation differs per tab.

Summary (7-day dots)

Each dot shows the combined mood score (1–3) of that day. The score is the average of all available components: emotion wheel score, mood grid valence (column 0–9 → 1–3) and energy average (0–100 → 1–3). Score 3 = green, 2 = orange, 1 = red.

Core feeling tab

Uses the combined mood score (same as summary). Score ≥ 3 = green, 2 = orange, 1 = red.

Green (3) joy, serenity, love, acceptance, trust, happiness, contentment, excitement, pride, gratitude, curiosity
Orange (2) surprise, anticipation, melancholy, anxiety
Red (1) sadness, anger, aggression, fear, disgust, shame, guilt

Mood tab

Based on the valence column (horizontal axis) of the 10×10 mood grid. Column 6–9 = green, 4–5 = orange, 0–3 = red.

Physical / Mental / Emotional tabs

Based on the slider value (0–100%) of that energy type. 67–100% = green, 34–66% = orange, 0–33% = red.
